About us

Our web platform is here to help and make a difference in this community by spreading cleanliness and raising awareness about the rubbish that is damaging our beautiful environment. We should make the planet a better, cleaner, and more refreshing place.

Mission

Our mission is to empower the improvement of our environment. We foster awareness, action, and sustainable solutions to greener future together as we flip through the pages of Trash Diaries.

Vision

Our vision, to aim and to create a world in which each object that is thrown away conveys a tale of accountability, rebirth, and thrift. We opt to change the negative connotations associated with garbage to positive ones that emphasize regrowth.
